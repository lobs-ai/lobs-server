# PAW Elevator Pitch v3

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-24

## 15-Second Version
PAW is your AI execution layer: it turns goals into clear tasks, routes work to the right agents, and helps you ship faster without losing control.

## 30-Second Version
Most people don’t need more ideas—they need consistent execution. PAW takes your goals, structures the work, and orchestrates progress across planning, writing, and delivery. You still approve key actions, but you stop carrying all the coordination overhead yourself.

## 60-Second Version
PAW helps individuals and small teams close the gap between intention and shipped outcomes. Instead of juggling disconnected tools, you work in one system that captures priorities, breaks work into actionable tasks, and delegates execution support through AI agents. It’s built for real-world constraints: limited time, context switching, and the need for accountability. You get speed and leverage, plus visibility into what was done, why, and what comes next.

## Role-Based Variants

### For Student Builder
PAW helps you turn limited time into visible progress. It gives your project structure, helps with writing and execution, and keeps you moving toward a launch.

### For Indie Founder
PAW acts like an execution multiplier for solo operators. It keeps strategy, tasks, and delivery connected so you can scale output before scaling headcount.

### For Busy Engineer
PAW reduces coordination drag. It automates repetitive workflow steps and documentation while preserving review points and technical control.
