## One-Line Claim

In one week, we converted a scattered backlog into shipped GTM assets, reclaiming **17 founder-hours** and reducing decision turnaround from **26 hours to 6 hours**.

## What Makes This Different

Most AI demos show generated text. This demo shows an **operating system**:

- Work intake became explicit tasks
- Tasks were routed by capability
- Human approvals stayed on high-risk decisions
- Outputs were shipped as reusable assets
- Impact was measured in workload and speed

- Ran multiple workstreams in parallel
