## SECTION 3: Solution — "Your AI Execution Layer"

### Section Header
**PAW handles the overhead so you can focus on the work only you can do.**

### Body Copy

PAW is not a chat assistant. It's not another project manager. It's an **execution layer** — the operating system underneath your work that turns goals into shipped outcomes.

Here's what changes:

**1. Goals become structured plans — automatically.**  
Describe what you want to accomplish. PAW breaks it into clear, scoped tasks with suggested next steps. No more blank-page project setups.

**2. Repetitive work gets routed — without your involvement.**  
Research, documentation drafts, coordination messages, summaries — PAW routes these to the right agent and delivers results you review, not tasks you execute.

**3. You stay in control of what matters.**  
High-impact decisions, final outputs, anything requiring your judgment — PAW surfaces these to you clearly, with context. Nothing gets shipped without your approval.

**4. Your work becomes visible and compound.**  
Decisions leave trails. Tasks close with artifacts. Nothing disappears into chat. PAW turns ephemeral effort into durable assets.

## SECTION 6: Objection Handling — FAQ

### Header
**Honest answers to the real questions.**

---

**"Isn't this just another AI tool that adds more complexity?"**  
PAW is designed to reduce the number of things you have to manage, not add another dashboard. You start with one goal. You get a clear plan and next actions. Most users are up and running in a single session.

---

**"Will PAW make decisions I don't want it to?"**  
No. PAW is a supervised execution layer with explicit approval points. High-impact steps — publishing, sending, committing — require your review. You define where the guardrails sit.

---

**"I'm already stretched thin. Does this take time to set up?"**  
Setup is one workflow. You describe a goal, PAW builds the plan, you approve. There is no process design work required on your end. Start with one project and expand from there.

---

**"How is this different from using ChatGPT or Notion AI?"**  
ChatGPT generates content when you ask. Notion AI edits documents. PAW executes workflows — it decomposes goals, routes work, tracks completion, and builds a persistent artifact record. It's the layer above chat.

---

**"Is my data safe?"**  
PAW is built with authentication-gated access, explicit data handling, and a clear permission model. Nothing runs without your credentials, and nothing persists without your approval.

## Copywriting Notes

**Voice:** Direct, technical-ish, no hype. Talk to a smart engineer who has heard too many AI pitches. Earn trust with specifics, not superlatives.

**Avoid:**
- "Revolutionary" / "game-changing" / "10x" without evidence
- Vague AI language ("harnesses the power of...")
- Feature lists without benefit translation

**Use:**
- Concrete metrics (hours, decisions, cycle time)
- Anchor to Rafe's specific situation — it's relatable and true
- Short sentences. One idea per sentence.
- Lead with outcomes, follow with how

**Conversion hypothesis:**
The primary drop-off on this page is trust — "will this actually work for someone like me?" Fix it by leading with the Agent-Built Week evidence pack, not the feature list.
